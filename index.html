FAQ

Why addaggio?
Because I never click on ads displayed in a search, I flick past ads I see on a webpage - I hate ads that spin and say 'look at me'. This is how I want to do it - I want to go to the ad I want! So I've made it a service for you too. No intrusion, no big brother snooping on your browsing... I don't even watch TV anymore. In my world, I choose... and now so can you.
What does addaggio do?
addaggio asks people what ads they want to see, and it delivers those ads to their inbox. The default is once a week, so you'll get a number of ads matching your preferences in one easy chunk. Or you can hop on and browse the site anytime without logging in.
What information does addaggio collect?
An email address and a password. If you want to tailor what ads you see, select categories or other filters. That's it. We don't care who you are and don't want to know. If you are an advertiser, we know you want to be known, so yes we collect some basic information that lets us do our job, like company name, contact person and number etc, website, email. We will never on-sell or share your information.
How much is it?
We simply charge advertisers $100 per week per ad. That's it. Regardless of how many times its seen. We're cheap to run so we're not going to break your bank either. That way the small players can join the big fish.
I want to advertise - how do I do that?
1. Register as an advertiser. 2. Placing an ad is as easy as filling out a few fields for copy, target demographic, adding a pic and a web destination so addaggio users can see more. 3. Say how long you want it to run for. That's it! After that it is delivered to anyone who matched the demographic you specified, both on site and in emails.
Can I opt-out?
addaggio gives you complete freedom. Just login and you'll see the option to stop using the service.
